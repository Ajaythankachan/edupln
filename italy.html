Study in Italy

It’s perhaps not surprising that many international students feel instantly at homein Italy. It's difficult to find a country as inspiring as Italy. Birthplace ofthe Renaissance, home of countless famous dishes and museums, and the hub ofthe best luxury brands, Italy is a world leader in many areas. Plus, it has unparalleled beauty in both architecture and nature  Today,Italy, the country of the world's greatest minds, welcomes bright students likeyou.  Thanks to its affordable andhigh-quality education, many international students choose Italy for theirstudies. Acountry with beauty everywhere, both in architecture and in nature, Italyprovides affordable and high-quality education in many fields. From north tosouth, the country is full of arts, culture, and history. And of course, greatuniversities that offer globally recognized degrees. 

Higher Education in Italy

Tuition fees

Tuitionfees for international students in Italy vary depending on the institution andprogram of study. On average, undergraduate programs cost around €1,000 to€3,000 per year, while graduate programs can cost between €1,500 to €5,000 peryear.

Why to Study in Italy?

Italy is a highly budget friendly country to study abroad compared to top study abroad destinations. The country houses one of the top business schools in Europe.

Why Public University in Italy?
  • ZERO TUITION FEES(Education is completely free for international students in public universities)
  • Fully Funded Scholarship (Scholarship amount is depend on the university the student is getting admitted to)
  • Bachelor's  3 years Scholarship & Masters 2 years Scholarship
  • Scholarship amount €7081 / 6,30,611 6 LAKH 30 THOUSAND ( Lazio Scholarship, as mention scholarship amount varies depend on the university and the municipality)
  • Scholarship amount will be credited to your personal bank account in Italy
  • Student will receive scholarship every year depend on their program (To apply for second year scholarship student must have 30 credit minimum in 2 semesters)
  • Cheapest  travel allowances
  • If the students have good scholarship ranking  Accommodation also will be provided by the Scholarship department, but they have to pay certain  amount ( which is much cheaper than taking rented houses)
  • Erasmus Scholarship programs available.
  • Stay back  1 year after graduation ( Stay back can be extended by late graduation up to 5 years).
  • Ielts or without Ielts
  • Schengen Country
Basic requirements for students & Visa documents
  • 75% Above ( Bachelors or 12th)
  • Diploma or Open school degree does not validate for requirement
  • ITR must be submitted by the student or either by one of the family member 
  • Good english communication skill
  • Year GAP after 12th (1 -2 years) & Bachelors (2 -3 years)
  • Year GAP must be filled with work experience in the same field of study
  • 6 months bank statement (Show money 12 lakh for masters & 17 Lakh). Money can be withdrawn after getting the visa.  If the ITR is from the family member half of the show money should be provided in the sponsor or family members account.
  • HRD attestation,
  • Apostle, Translation & Legalization
  • DOV (Declaration of Value)
  • Travel Insurance
  • Accommodation Proof
  • Confirmed Air Tickets ( With PNR)
  • 4 types of Scholarship Documents ( Apostle, Translation & Legalization)
